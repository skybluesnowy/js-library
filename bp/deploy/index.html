Blind Pig

Rumors Persist

There's a new tipple at the speakeasies. Or so they say. Cider with fruit and spirit flavours.

Thought I saw a bottle, at a corner table. Some sharp guy and his dolled up dame.

So I ask the barman. He says, 'Blind Pig? What Blind Pig?'

Cider for insiders, so it seems.

Does it exist? Depends who's asking.
